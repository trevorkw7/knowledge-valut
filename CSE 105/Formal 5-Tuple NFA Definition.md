$(Q, \Sigma, \delta, q_{0}, F)$

	- $\Sigma$ alphabet
	- $\delta: Q \times \Sigma_{\epsilon} \rightarrow P(Q)$ is the transition function (described by table)
	- $q_{0} \in Q$ is the start state
	- $F \subset Q$ is the set of accept states
